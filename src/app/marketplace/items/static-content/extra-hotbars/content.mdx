# Extra Hotbars

Are you tired of constantly switching between hotbars? Look no further than Extra Hotbars! With this marketplace item, you'll receive 3 additional hotbars, bringing your total to 6. That's enough space for up to 60 items right at your fingertips!

No more opening your inventory to search for the item you need or wasting valuable building time. With Extra Hotbars, you'll have all your tools and materials readily available. Whether you're constructing a massive machine or a tiny detail, you'll be able to quickly switch between your hotbars and get the job done with ease.

Please note that swapping between hotbars is only available in Creative Mode. Don't let a lack of hotbar space hold you back from your creative potential. Upgrade your Scrap Mechanic experience today with Extra Hotbars!
